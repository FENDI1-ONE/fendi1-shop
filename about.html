About FENDI1

Spotify-Bio: FENDI1 is a rap artist from germany. His sound lives somewhere between raw rage and atmospheric melancholy. His music hits hard with distorted 808s but always carries emotion - a balance between chaos and clarity. Whether it's the melodic shadows of LOVE&DRUGS or the glitchy energy of u miss me?... FENDI1 isn't following trends - he's shaping his own lane. Influenced by opium aesthetics, Lo-Fi rap and Hyperpop, he belnds genres to build a sonic world that feels both futuristic and deeply personal. His voice floats over heavy production, giving every track a mood that lingers.
